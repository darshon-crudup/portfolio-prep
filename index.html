I picked this particular skill because it can benefit my personal entrepreneurial goals and open up the doors for job opportunities. I think my ideal environment would be in software, creative media, or security. My previous graphic design experiences give me a boost in my new career. I've always had a natural eye to create and produce good visuals. It is this unique combination of creativity and design I possess which may give me a slight edge on my new career in tech.
